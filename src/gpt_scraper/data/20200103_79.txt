U.S. Strike Against Iran Rattles Markets
The world was shaken as news broke of a U.S. military strike against Iran, sending shockwaves through global markets. The strike, which occurred in the early hours of the morning, targeted key military installations in Iran, marking a significant escalation in tensions between the two nations.

The stock markets, which had been enjoying a period of relative stability, experienced a sharp downturn, with major indices plummeting as uncertainty and fear gripped investors. Safe-haven assets such as gold and government bonds saw a surge in demand as investors sought refuge from the market volatility.
