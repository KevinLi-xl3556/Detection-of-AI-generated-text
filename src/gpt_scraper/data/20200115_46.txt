David Attenborough, the legendary broadcaster and naturalist, is back with a new documentary series that is sure to captivate audiences around the world. Titled “David Attenborough: The Walrus and the Whistleblower,” the series follows the story of a former marine mammal trainer who takes on one of the most powerful companies in the world to expose animal abuse.
The documentary is set in Canada, where a colony of Pacific walruses has been brought to a cramped and unnatural environment in an aquarium.
