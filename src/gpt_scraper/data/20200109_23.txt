Are you looking for a good laugh this weekend? Well, you’re in luck because New York City has some of the best comedy shows in the world. From stand-up to improv, there’s something for everyone. Here are four comedy shows you won’t want to miss in N.Y.C. this weekend.


Comedy Cellar
The Comedy Cellar is a legendary venue that has hosted some of the biggest names in comedy, including Chris Rock, Jerry Seinfeld, and Dave Chappelle. The club has multiple shows each night, and you never know who might drop in for a surprise set. The atmosphere is intimate and cozy, perfect for a night of laughter with friends.


Upright Citizens Brigade
If you’re a fan of improv comedy, then you’ll love Upright Citizens Brigade. Founded by Amy Poehler, Matt Besser, Ian Roberts, and Matt Walsh, UCB has become a breeding ground for some of the most talented comedians in the industry. Their shows are always hilarious and unpredictable, with some of the best improv teams in the city taking the stage.


The Stand Comedy Club
Located in the heart of Union Square, The Stand Comedy Club is a popular spot for both established and up-and-coming comedians. They have a great selection of craft beers and cocktails, as well as a menu of delicious food to enjoy during the show. The club has a spacious layout and a friendly atmosphere, making it the perfect spot for a night out with friends.


Comedy at The Knitting Factory
The Knitting Factory is a unique venue that combines comedy and music in one space. Their comedy shows feature a mix of local and national talent, with performances ranging from stand-up to sketch comedy. The venue is known for its laid-back vibe and cozy atmosphere, making it a great spot for a night out with friends.


No matter what kind of comedy you’re into, there’s something for everyone in N.Y.C. this weekend. So grab some friends, head to one of these venues, and get ready to laugh your way through the night. You won’t regret it!
