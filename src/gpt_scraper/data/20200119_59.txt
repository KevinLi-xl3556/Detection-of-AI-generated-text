Television lovers, get ready for an exciting Sunday night as two gripping shows, ‘Avenue 5’ and ‘9-1-1: Lone Star’ are set to make your night worth remembering.
‘Avenue 5’ is an American science fiction comedy television series created by Armando Iannucci, known for his work on the popular HBO series ‘Veep’. The show premiered in January 2020 and has been appreciated for its unique blend of humor and science fiction elements. The show follows the story of a luxurious space cruise ship, Avenue 5, which gets thrown off course, resulting in panic and chaos among its passengers. The show is set to air its eighth episode on Sunday at 10 p.m. ET on HBO.
Following ‘Avenue 5’, ‘9-1-1: Lone Star’ is set to air at 8 p.m. ET on FOX. Created by Ryan Murphy and Brad Falchuk, the show is a spin-off of the popular series ‘9-1-1’. The show premiered in January 2020 and stars Rob Lowe as the captain of the firefighting crew of the 126, a station in Austin, Texas that is composed entirely of firefighters who, to varying degrees, struggle with personal or professional problems. The show has been appreciated for its diverse cast and thrilling storyline that revolves around the challenges faced by firefighters in their line of duty.
Both ‘Avenue 5’ and ‘9-1-1: Lone Star’ have been hailed as must-watch shows due to their gripping storylines, impressive performances, and unique blend of genres. These shows are perfect for anyone who loves a good laugh and enjoys watching thrilling action sequences.
