Iran:
After the US killed Iran’s top general Qasem Soleimani in a drone strike on January 3, Iran retaliated by attacking two US bases in Iraq. Grammy Awards:
On Sunday, the 62nd annual Grammy Awards will be held at the Staples Center in Los Angeles. The event, which recognizes excellence in the music industry, will be hosted by Alicia Keys. Some of the evening’s performers include Ariana Grande, Demi Lovato, and Billie Eilish, who is nominated in six categories.
Carlos Beltran:
New York Mets manager Carlos Beltran has resigned from his position amid allegations of involvement in the Houston Astros sign-stealing scandal. The scandal, which has rocked the baseball world, involves stealing signs from opposing teams during the 2017 season.
