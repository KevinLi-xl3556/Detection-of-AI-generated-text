If you’re looking for some entertainment in New York City this weekend, you’re in luck. The city that never sleeps has a multitude of plays and musicals to choose from. Whether you’re in the mood for a classic Broadway show or something new and cutting-edge, there’s sure to be something that catches your eye.
Here are 15 plays and musicals to consider checking out this weekend:


Hamilton - This Tony Award-winning musical has been a hit since it opened on Broadway in 2015. Follow the story of founding father Alexander Hamilton in this hip-hop-infused retelling of American history.


Jagged Little Pill - This musical is based on the iconic album by Alanis Morissette and deals with issues such as addiction, sexual assault, and family dynamics.


Wicked - This Broadway classic tells the story of the witches of Oz before Dorothy arrived, and how they became the characters we know from The Wizard of Oz.


Moulin Rouge! The Musical - This musical adaptation of the hit movie features an eclectic soundtrack and stunning costumes and choreography.


Waitress - Follow the story of a waitress and expert pie maker as she navigates life and love in this heartwarming musical.


To Kill a Mockingbird - Based on the classic novel by Harper Lee, this play explores themes of racism and injustice in the American South.


Come From Away - This musical tells the heartwarming story of how the residents of a small town in Canada welcomed thousands of stranded travelers after the 9/11 attacks.


Hadestown - This Tony Award-winning musical reimagines the Greek myth of Orpheus and Eurydice in a modern, jazz-inspired style.


The Lion King - This Broadway classic tells the story of young Simba as he navigates life in the African savannah and learns to become a king.


Aladdin - This musical adaptation of the Disney classic features beloved songs such as “A Whole New World” and stunning set designs.


Company - This revival of Stephen Sondheim’s classic musical features a gender-swapped cast and explores themes of love and commitment.


Six - This musical retells the story of Henry VIII’s six wives, each with their own unique voice and style.


Mrs. Doubtfire - Based on the hit movie, this musical follows a divorced father as he disguises himself as a Scottish nanny to spend more time with his kids.


Diana: The Musical - This new musical explores the life of Princess Diana and the impact she had on the world.


Little Shop of Horrors - This classic musical comedy tells the story of a hapless florist and his carnivorous plant.


No matter what your interests are, there’s sure to be a play or musical that suits your fancy. So why not take a break from the hustle and bustle of the city and immerse yourself in the magic of live theater? With so many great options to choose from, you’re sure to have a weekend to remember.
