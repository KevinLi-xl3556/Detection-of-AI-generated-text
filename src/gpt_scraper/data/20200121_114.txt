First, let’s start with the basics. “Derring-do” is a noun that refers to “brave and adventurous feats”. It’s often used to describe someone who is daring and courageous, who is not afraid to take risks and face challenges head-on. The phrase originated from the Middle English term “derrynge do”, which means “boldness and audacity in combat”.
