Winter is here, and what better way to cozy up inside than by binging on some great television shows? Whether you’re looking for a drama, comedy, or something in between, there are plenty of options to choose from. In this article, we’ll be highlighting 50 TV shows you need to watch this winter.


Succession - This gripping drama follows the Roy family, who control one of the biggest media and entertainment conglomerates in the world. As they battle for power and influence, things get heated and intense.


Ted Lasso - A heartwarming comedy about an American football coach who moves to England to manage a struggling soccer team. With its charming characters and witty writing, Ted Lasso is a feel-good hit.


The Crown - This historical drama chronicles the reign of Queen Elizabeth II, from her early days on the throne to more recent events. With an all-star cast and stunning production values, The Crown is a must-watch.


The Handmaid’s Tale - Set in a dystopian future where women are treated as property, this drama follows a woman’s struggle to survive and escape from the oppressive regime.


Sex Education - This British comedy-drama follows a teenager who sets up a sex therapy clinic at his school to help his classmates with their love lives. With its quirky characters and frank discussions about sex, Sex Education is both hilarious and insightful.


Yellowstone - This Western drama follows the Dutton family, who control the largest contiguous ranch in the United States. With its stunning vistas and gripping storylines, Yellowstone is a must-watch for fans of the genre.


Squid Game - This Korean survival drama has taken the world by storm, thanks to its intense storyline and unexpected twists. In the show, contestants compete in a series of deadly games to win a cash prize.


Mare of Easttown - This crime drama stars Kate Winslet as a detective investigating a murder in a small Pennsylvania town. With its gritty realism and stunning performances, Mare of Easttown is a must-watch.


Stranger Things - Set in the 1980s, this sci-fi horror series follows a group of friends as they uncover dark secrets and supernatural phenomena in their small town. With its nostalgic vibe and lovable characters, Stranger Things is a hit with audiences of all ages.


Atlanta - This critically acclaimed comedy-drama follows the lives of two cousins as they navigate the Atlanta music scene. With its sharp writing and standout performances, Atlanta is a must-watch for fans of the genre.


The Boys - This superhero series takes a dark and gritty look at what happens when superheroes abuse their powers. With its intense action and dark humor, The Boys is a hit with fans of the genre.


Euphoria - This drama follows a group of high school students as they navigate love, sex, and addiction. With its bold storytelling and standout performances, Euphoria is a must-watch.


The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel - Set in the 1950s, this comedy follows a housewife who discovers she has a talent for stand-up comedy. With its witty writing and stunning production values, The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel is a hit with fans of the genre.


Fargo - This crime anthology series tells a different story each season, all set in the same world as the Coen Brothers’ classic film of the same name. With its quirky characters and dark humor, Fargo is a must-watch for fans of the genre.


Ozark - This crime drama follows a family who move to the Ozarks to launder money for a drug cartel. With its intense storylines and standout performances, Ozark is a hit with audiences.
