Inherit the Viper is a 2019 crime thriller directed by Anthony Jerjen and written by Andrew Crabtree. The film stars Josh Hartnett, Margarita Levieva, and Owen Teague. It tells the story of three siblings in a poverty-stricken town in Appalachia who are struggling to make ends meet by selling drugs. The movie is a gritty portrayal of the harsh realities of life in an economically deprived community.
The movie is set in a small town in Appalachia, where the economic opportunities are scarce, and people are struggling to survive. Kip (Josh Hartnett), his sister Josie (Margarita Levieva), and younger brother Boots (Owen Teague) have inherited a drug-dealing business from their deceased father. They are all struggling to keep the business going while dealing with the daily challenges of life in a poverty-stricken town.
Inherit the Viper is a story about the harsh realities of life in a hard town. The movie is a slow-burning crime thriller that builds tension as the story progresses. The characters are well-developed, and the actors deliver excellent performances.
