As the week begins, it’s time to start planning out your meals for the days ahead. Whether you’re looking for quick and easy recipes or something a little more elaborate, there are plenty of delicious dishes to choose from. Here are some ideas for what to cook this week:

Grilled Chicken with Roasted Vegetables

This simple and healthy meal is perfect for a weeknight dinner. Start by marinating some chicken breasts in your favorite marinade for at least an hour. Then, grill them until they’re cooked through. While the chicken is grilling, roast some vegetables in the oven, such as bell peppers, onions, and zucchini. Serve the chicken and vegetables together for a tasty and nutritious meal.

Shrimp Scampi Linguine

This classic Italian dish is perfect for a special occasion or a date night at home. Start by cooking the linguine according to package instructions. While the pasta is cooking, sauté some shrimp in butter, garlic, and white wine. Toss the cooked linguine with the shrimp and sauce, and serve with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese and fresh parsley.

Vegetarian Tacos

If you’re looking for a meatless meal option, try making vegetarian tacos. Start by sautéing some diced onions and bell peppers in a skillet. Add a can of black beans and some taco seasoning, and let the mixture simmer for a few minutes. Serve the bean mixture in taco shells, and top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and shredded cheese.

Slow-Cooker Beef Stew

This hearty and comforting meal is perfect for a chilly evening. Start by browning some cubed beef in a skillet. Then, transfer the beef to a slow cooker along with diced potatoes, carrots, and onions. Add beef broth, tomato paste, and seasonings, and let the stew cook on low for several hours. Serve with crusty bread for dipping.

Lemon Garlic Salmon

This flavorful and healthy meal is perfect for a light dinner. Start by marinating some salmon fillets in lemon juice and minced garlic for at least 30 minutes. Then, bake the salmon in the oven until it’s cooked through. Serve with a side of roasted vegetables or a fresh salad.
These are just a few ideas for what to cook this week. Whether you’re looking for something quick and easy or something a little more involved, there are plenty of delicious meals to choose from. Happy cooking!
