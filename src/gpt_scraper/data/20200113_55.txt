The 2020 Oscar-nominated movies have created a buzz among the cinephiles who appreciate the craft of filmmaking. To watch these fantastic movies in the comfort of your home during this pandemic, you would need to know where to watch them. Here is the list of the movies that received nominations, their streaming services, and a brief on their storyline:


Parasite – This South Korean thriller directed by Bong Joon-ho won four Academy Awards, including Best Picture, Best Director, Best Original Screenplay, and Best International Feature Film. The movie revolves around a poor family, the Kims, who infiltrate the household of the wealthy Park family. Parasite is available on Hulu.


Joker – Directed by Todd Phillips, this psychological thriller starring Joaquin Phoenix as the infamous DC Comics villain, Joker, received eleven nominations and won two Academy Awards - Best Actor and Best Original Score. The movie follows Arthur Fleck, a failed comedian who becomes the Clown Prince of Crime. Joker is available on HBO Max.


Once Upon a Time in Hollywood – This Quentin Tarantino movie set in the 1960s starring Leonardo DiCaprio and Brad Pitt has been nominated for ten awards and won two - Best Supporting Actor for Brad Pitt and Best Production Design. It tells the story of an aging actor and his stunt double trying to make it big in Hollywood. Once Upon a Time in Hollywood is available on Starz.


Little Women – This movie, directed by Greta Gerwig, earned six nominations and won one for Best Costume Design. Little Women is based on Louisa May Alcott’s novel and follows the lives of four sisters, Meg, Jo, Beth, and Amy, as they navigate their journey to womanhood in 1860s New England. Little Women is available on Starz.


Marriage Story – Directed by Noah Baumbach, this movie tells the story of a couple, Charlie and Nicole, as they navigate their divorce and co-parenting. The movie received six nominations and won one for Best Supporting Actress for Laura Dern. Marriage Story is available on Netflix.


The Irishman – Directed by the legendary Martin Scorsese, this movie starring Robert De Niro, Al Pacino, and Joe Pesci has been nominated for ten awards. The Irishman is a crime epic that tells the story of Frank Sheeran, a mob hitman who claimed to have killed Teamster boss Jimmy Hoffa. The Irishman is available on Netflix.


These Oscar-nominated movies are a must-watch for anyone who loves the art of filmmaking. With the streaming services mentioned above, you can enjoy these movies in the comfort of your own home. So sit back, grab some popcorn, and be mesmerized by these cinematic masterpieces.
