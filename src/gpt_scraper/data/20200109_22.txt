The Film Forum, located in West Houston Street, is showing the classic film series “The Godfather” this weekend. Beginning on Friday and running through Sunday, the trilogy follows the Corleone family as they navigate the world of organized crime in America. The series is a must-see for any cinema enthusiast, with award-winning performances and iconic scenes that are still referenced in pop culture today.


“Harry Potter” Series at the Museum of the Moving Image:
The Museum of the Moving Image, located in Astoria, Queens, is showing all eight of the “Harry Potter” films this weekend. Beginning on Saturday and running through Monday, the series will transport you to the magical world of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The films are a great way to escape reality for a few hours and immerse yourself in a fantastical world filled with wizards, dragons, and spells.


“The Lord of the Rings” Trilogy at the Alamo Drafthouse:
The Alamo Drafthouse, located in Downtown Brooklyn, is showing the epic “The Lord of the Rings” film series this weekend. Beginning on Friday and running through Sunday, the trilogy follows hobbit Frodo Baggins and his companions as they embark on a dangerous journey to destroy the powerful ring of Sauron. The films are a cinematic masterpiece, with stunning visual effects and an unforgettable score that will leave you breathless.
