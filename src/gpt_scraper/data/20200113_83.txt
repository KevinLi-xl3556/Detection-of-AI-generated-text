Soup’s On! A Delicious Guide to Comforting Soups
When the weather turns chilly, there’s nothing quite like a warm bowl of soup to lift your spirits and warm your bones. Soups are one of the most versatile and comforting foods around, with endless flavor combinations and variations to suit any palate. Whether you’re in the mood for something creamy and indulgent, or something light and healthy, there’s a soup out there that’s perfect for you.
One of the best things about soups is that they’re incredibly easy to make. Even if you’re not a confident cook, you can whip up a delicious pot of soup in no time. All you need is a few basic ingredients, some stock or broth, and a little bit of time. You can even make a big batch of soup and freeze it for later, so you always have a comforting meal on hand.
Here are some of our favorite soup recipes to get you started:


Classic Chicken Noodle Soup - This is the ultimate comfort food soup, perfect for when you’re feeling under the weather or just need a little pick-me-up. Start with a homemade chicken broth, then add plenty of noodles, carrots, celery, and chunks of tender chicken. Don’t forget to sprinkle in some fresh herbs like thyme and parsley for added flavor.


Creamy Tomato Soup - This soup is like a hug in a bowl. Start by sautéing onions and garlic in butter, then add canned tomatoes, vegetable broth, and a splash of cream. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until it’s smooth and creamy, then serve with a grilled cheese sandwich for the ultimate comfort food pairing.


Spicy Sausage and Kale Soup - If you’re in the mood for something with a little kick, this soup is perfect. Brown some spicy Italian sausage in a pot, then add onion, garlic, and plenty of chopped kale. Add chicken broth and let the soup simmer until the flavors meld together. Serve with a crusty piece of bread for a satisfying meal.


Butternut Squash Soup - This soup is perfect for fall, when butternut squash is in season. Roast the squash with some olive oil and spices until it’s tender, then puree it with vegetable broth until it’s smooth. Add some coconut milk for richness, and garnish with toasted pepitas and a drizzle of honey.


Lentil Soup - This soup is packed with protein and fiber, making it a healthy and filling option. Start by sautéing onions, carrots, and celery in a pot, then add lentils, canned tomatoes, and vegetable broth. Let the soup simmer until the lentils are tender, then garnish with fresh parsley and a squeeze of lemon.


Whether you’re looking for something hearty and comforting or light and healthy, there’s a soup out there for you. So grab a ladle and get cooking - soup’s on!
