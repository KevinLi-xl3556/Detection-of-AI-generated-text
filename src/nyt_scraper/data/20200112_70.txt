Nicole Guerra, Sean LaRochelle
Nicole Guerra and Sean LaRochelle were married Jan. 11 at the Club of Knights in Coral Gables, Fla. The Rev. Frank Nunez, who was ordained in the St. Pio of Pietrelcina Orthodox Catholic Apostolic Church, officiated.
The bride, 25, handles check processing and mortgage services for the Mineo Salcedo Law Firm in Davie, Fla.
She is a daughter of Martha M. Guerra and Ramon M. Guerra of Miami. The bride’s father is a mortgage loan originator for Caliber Home Loans in Miami. Her mother is an executive vice president at US Century Bank, also in Miami.
The groom, 24, is a lead rental car sales associate at Avis Budget in Fort Lauderdale, Fla.
He is the son of Iraci Meyenberg of Miami and Claude LaRochelle of Atlanta. The groom’s mother is a kindergarten teacher at Trinitas Academy, a private Christian school in Plantation, Fla. His father, a retired police detective in Miami-Dade, Fla., is now a store manager at O’Reilly Auto Parts in Atlanta.
The couple met in 2015 at a gas station in Pembroke Pines, Fla.
