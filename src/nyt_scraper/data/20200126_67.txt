Quotation of the Day: 75 Years After the Liberation of Auschwitz, a Fear That ‘Never Again’ Is Not Assured
“I cannot say it will never happen again, because when you look at some leaders of today, those dangerous ambitions, pride and sense of being better than others are still at play. Who knows where they can lead.”
ZOFIA POSMYSZ, a 96-year-old Polish survivor of Auschwitz.
