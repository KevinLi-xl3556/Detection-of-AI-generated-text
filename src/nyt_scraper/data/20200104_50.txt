Quotation of the Day: Pulling the Trigger on a Target Who Didn’t Hide
“Suleimani was treated like royalty, and was not particularly hard to find. Suleimani absolutely felt untouchable, particularly in Iraq.”
MARC POLYMEROPOULOS, a former senior C.I.A. operations officer, on the United States strike that killed Maj. Gen. Qassim Suleimani, Iran’s top security and intelligence commander.
