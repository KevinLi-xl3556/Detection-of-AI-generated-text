Alexandra Quitko, Adam Baranski
Alexandra Joy Quitko and Adam Craig Baranski were married Jan. 25 at the Miami Beach Edition, a hotel in Miami Beach. Rabbi Jonathan Fisch officiated.
Mrs. Baranski, 29, is a wealth adviser at Omnia Family Wealth, a wealth management firm in Aventura, Fla. She graduated from Tulane, from which she also received a master’s degree in accounting.
She is the daughter of Francine G. Quitko of Boca Raton, Fla., and the late Alan G. Quitko. The bride’s mother works in Boca Raton as a luxury manager for the RealReal, the consignment site based in San Francisco. The bride’s father was the owner of Quitko Realty Corporation, a commercial real estate company that was located in New York.
Mr. Baranski, 34, is a regional marketing director at Southern Glazer’s Wine and Spirits, a distributor in Miami. He graduated from Indiana University and received an M.B.A. from the Florida International University.
He is the son of Janis B. Baranski and Robert I. Baranski of Weston, Fla. The groom’s mother is a stay-at-home parent. His father is the managing partner for Brittany Sales, a wine and spirits company based in Miami.
The couple met in 2017 through JDate.
