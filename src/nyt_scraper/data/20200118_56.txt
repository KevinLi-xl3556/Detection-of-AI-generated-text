Quotation of the Day: Tech Giants ‘Bullied’ Us, Rivals Testify
“It’s like playing a soccer game.You might be the best team in the league, but you’re playing against a team that owns the field, the ball, the stadium and the entire league, and they can change the rules of the game in their own favor and anytime.”
KIRSTEN DARU, the vice president and general counsel of Tile, which makes small tracking devices, on how tech giants have used their power to squeeze out much smaller rivals.
