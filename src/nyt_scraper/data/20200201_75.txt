Corrections: Feb. 1, 2020
An article on Thursday about Kryptos, a courtyard sculpture  at C.I.A.  headquarters, misstated the decryption at the end of Passage Two. The decryption of those letters is “XLAYERTWO,” not “LEVELTWO.”
An article on Friday about proposed tweaks to the Volcker Rule misstated the name of a trade group. It is the Independent Community Bankers of America, not the Independent Community Bankers Association.
A picture caption with an article on Page 7 misspelled the surname of a dancer. He is Max Westwell, not Westwall. It also misstated his age. He is 34, not 33.
An article on Page 17 about the final season of “Homeland” misspelled the given name of an actor in the series. He is Damian Lewis, not Damien.

Errors are corrected during the press run whenever possible, so some errors noted here may not have appeared in all editions.
