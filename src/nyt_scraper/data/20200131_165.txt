Quotation of the Day: Iowa Could Make or Break Biden’s War Chest
“Many people win the Daytona 500 on fumes, and many people lose badly with a full tank of gas. That’s the nature of auto racing and that’s the same nature of politics.”
JOHN MORGAN, a Florida fund-raiser for former Vice President Joseph R. Biden Jr., on the possibility that a candidate can still come out on top in a political race despite being badly outspent by opponents.
