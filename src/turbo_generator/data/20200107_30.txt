T's Favorite Nourishing Recipes for a More Healthful 2020

New Year, new you? Many of us start off the year with the goal of improving our health and wellness, but sometimes it can be difficult to know where to start. Luckily, T's favorite nourishing recipes can help jump-start your journey to a healthier year.

First up is a hearty lentil soup packed with protein, fiber and essential nutrients. This soup is not only healthy, but also easy to make and budget-friendly. Simply sauté onions and garlic, add in carrots and celery, and then add in lentils, vegetable broth and spices. Let it simmer for about 40 minutes, and you have a delicious and nutritious meal. Serve with a side of whole grain bread for a complete and satisfying meal.

Another great recipe is a quinoa and vegetable stir-fry. Quinoa is an excellent source of protein and fiber, and it pairs perfectly with veggies for a satisfying meal. Simply cook quinoa according to package directions, and then sauté colorful vegetables like bell peppers, zucchini and mushrooms in a hot skillet. Add in spices and a drizzle of olive oil, and then toss in the cooked quinoa. This dish is perfect for a quick and healthy dinner, or as a tasty lunch for the next day.

For a sweet treat that won't derail your healthy goals, try T's favorite chia seed pudding. Chia seeds are loaded with nutrients like omega-3 fatty acids and antioxidants, and they make a great base for a creamy and indulgent pudding. Simply mix chia seeds, almond milk, vanilla extract, and a touch of honey in a bowl or jar and let it sit in the refrigerator for a few hours or overnight. Top with fresh fruit or a sprinkle of nuts for added texture and flavor.

By incorporating these nourishing recipes into your 2020 meal plan, you'll be well on your way to a healthier, more balanced and enjoyable diet. So, grab your apron and get cooking - your body (and taste buds) will thank you!
