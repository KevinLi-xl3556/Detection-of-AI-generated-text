As the world grows increasingly concerned with climate change and its impact on our planet, the automotive industry is responding with a surge in electric vehicle (EV) production. These vehicles are not only cleaner and more environmentally friendly, but they also offer a unique and exciting driving experience. With new EVs being introduced to the market every year, it can be tough to keep track of which ones are worth watching. In this article, we’ll take a look at 10 of the most promising electric vehicles to watch out for in the coming months and beyond.

1. Tesla Model 3: This highly anticipated vehicle has been dubbed the “affordable” Tesla, with a starting price of $35,000. It’s set to hit the market in late 2017, and has already received over 400,000 pre-orders. The Model 3 boasts a range of 215 miles on a single charge, and has all the features and sleek design that Tesla is known for.

2. Chevrolet Bolt: This compact hatchback is poised to disrupt the EV market with its impressive 238 miles of range and affordable price point. The Bolt is already available for purchase in the US, and is expected to be released in Europe and Asia in the near future.

3. Audi e-tron quattro: The German automaker is set to release its first all-electric SUV in 2018, with a range of over 300 miles. It’s said to have fast charging capabilities, and its sleek design will no doubt appeal to luxury car buyers.

4. Faraday Future FF 91: This luxury EV caused quite a stir when it was unveiled at the 2017 Consumer Electronics Show. While the company is facing financial troubles, if the car does make it to production it will boast impressive features like facial recognition software and autonomous driving capabilities.

5. BMW i3: BMW’s compact EV has been on the market for a few years now, but a 2017 model update saw its range increase to 114 miles. It’s still one of the most stylish and distinctive electric vehicles on the road.

6. Nissan Leaf: The Leaf has been around since 2010, but recent upgrades have improved its range to 107 miles. Nissan is reportedly working on a next-generation Leaf with a range of over 200 miles.

7. Tesla Model X: This all-electric SUV from Tesla has gull-wing doors, and a range of up to 295 miles. Its futuristic design and impressive performance have made it a hit with luxury car buyers, despite the hefty price tag.

8. Porsche Mission E: Porsche’s first all-electric vehicle promises blistering performance, with a 0-60 mph time of under 3.5 seconds. It’s slated to arrive in 2019, and with an expected range of over 300 miles, it’s likely to give Tesla a run for its money.

9. Hyundai Ioniq: This hybrid sedan is available in electric, plug-in hybrid, and hybrid versions. The electric model has a range of 124 miles, and is one of the more affordable options on this list.

10. Jaguar I-PACE: Jaguar’s first all-electric vehicle is a sleek and stylish SUV with a range of over 300 miles. It’s set to hit the market in late 2018, and has already generated a lot of buzz.

The electric vehicle market is growing rapidly, and these 10 vehicles are just the tip of the iceberg. With companies like Volvo, Mercedes-Benz, and others investing heavily in EV development, we can expect even more exciting vehicles to hit the market in the near future. Whether you’re looking for luxury or affordability, speed or range, there’s an electric vehicle out there that’s perfect for you.
