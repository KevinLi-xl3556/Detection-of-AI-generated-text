As we enter a new year, many of us have resolved to eat more healthily in order to nourish ourselves and feel better. But with so many diet plans and fads out there, it can be hard to know where to start. Fear not, as T Magazine has you covered with some of our favourite nourishing recipes for a more healthful 2020.

1. Roasted Cauliflower and Chickpea Salad - This hearty and satisfying salad is a great source of plant-based protein, packed with flavourful spices and roasted to crispy perfection.

2. Turmeric and Ginger Soup - Turmeric and ginger are both known for their anti-inflammatory properties, making this soup a perfect choice for those looking to soothe an upset stomach or reduce inflammation.

3. Lentil and Spinach Curry - This vegan curry is easy to make and full of healthy ingredients. Lentils are a great source of plant-based protein, while spinach is rich in antioxidants and vitamins.

4. Broiled Salmon with Lemon and Thyme - Salmon is an excellent source of omega-3 fatty acids, which are important for heart health. This simple recipe is easy to make and bursting with flavour.

5. Quinoa and Black Bean Veggie Burgers - These burgers are packed with protein and fibre, making them a satisfying and healthy meal option. The quinoa adds a nutty flavour and the black beans add a delicious creaminess.

6. Roasted Sweet Potato and Wild Rice Salad - Sweet potatoes are a great source of fibre and vitamins, and wild rice adds a satisfying crunch. This salad is a perfect lunchtime option that will leave you feeling full and energized.

7. Almond Butter and Banana Smoothie - This smoothie is a perfect breakfast option for those who are short on time. Almond butter adds protein and healthy fats, while bananas provide natural sweetness and energy.
